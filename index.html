About Us

School


East Delhi Public School, Pratap Vihar started its journey in 2006 and made a niche for itself in the hearts and minds of the people throughout Ghaziabad in a very short span of time. The School has been growing in strength and enjoying achievements in all directions of educational endeavors from academics to sport and from music and dance to computers. Efforts of the students are noteworthy as it is they who have brought name and fame to the school. Above all there is a focus on value education and a renewed effort to pursue and enrich our basic moral values and cultural heritage coupled with a sense of social responsibility.
